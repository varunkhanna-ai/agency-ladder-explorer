# DeliverEase Refund Policy

## 1. Cold Food / Quality Issues
If your food arrives cold or does not meet quality standards, you are eligible for a refund coupon. 
- **Refund Window:** You must file a complaint within **60 minutes** of delivery.
- **Proof Required:** You **must** provide a photo of the food as proof.
- **Refund Cap:** Up to the order total or $20, whichever is lower.

## 2. Delivery Delay Compensation
We strive to deliver on time. If your order is significantly delayed, we offer compensation.
- **Definition of Delay:** An order is considered delayed if it arrives more than 15 minutes past the promised time.
- **Proof Required:** No photo proof is required for delivery delays. Our system tracks GPS and time automatically.
- **Refund Cap:** A flat $5 coupon for delays between 15–30 minutes, and $10 for delays over 30 minutes.

## 3. Missing Items
If items are missing from your order:
- **Refund Window:** File within 30 minutes of delivery.
- **Proof Required:** No photo required, but please check the bag thoroughly.
- **Refund Amount:** Full refund for the missing item(s).

## 4. Damaged Packaging
If your packaging is severely damaged:
- **Refund Window:** File within 30 minutes of delivery.
- **Proof Required:** Photo of the damaged packaging.
- **Refund Amount:** Up to 50% of the order total, max $10.

## 5. What Is NOT Covered
- Payment disputes (contact your bank).
- Change of mind after ordering.
- Incorrect address provided by the customer.
- Allergic reactions (please see our escalation policy for medical emergencies).

## 6. Coupon Caps and Limits
- The maximum refund coupon value is **$20 USD**.
- Coupons are issued as DeliverEase credits and expire in 30 days.
- Multiple small refunds may be capped if they exceed 50% of your total order history value in a month.
